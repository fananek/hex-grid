Structure Attribute​Encodable 

public struct AttributeEncodable: Encodable

A type-erased Encodable value.

The AttributeEncodable type forwards encoding responsibilities to an underlying value, hiding its specific underlying type.

You can encode mixed-type values in dictionaries and other collections that require Encodable conformance by declaring their contained type to be AttributeEncodable.
